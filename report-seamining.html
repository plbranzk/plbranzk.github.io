Deep Sea Mining – Impact Assessment Review

Goal

This report critically evaluates the quality of Environmental and Social Impact Assessments (EIA/SIA) submitted by the NORI deep-sea mining project. It informs the International Seabed Authority (ISA) on potential gaps in current assessment processes and provides recommendations for improving future regulatory frameworks, especially ahead of commercial mining decisions.

Methods

The analysis uses a twofold approach:

  • Process evaluation – focuses on stakeholder engagement, procedural justice, and informed consent.
  • Content evaluation – assesses assumptions in risk scoping, adequacy of impact models, and robustness of monitoring systems.
Supporting tools include a stakeholder power-interest grid, timeline reconstruction, and cross-validation with ISA guidelines and academic literature.
